Venue

At Community Science Museum we can offer a private venue for your birthday party or a conference room for a job gathering for you and your colleagues.

You find more about the offers below and you are welcome to contact us if you are interested to book or got more questions.

Contact us

Celebrate your birthday party with science!

We arrange a separate room for you and your friends with cake, soft drinks and whatever may be wanted on the menu.

In the birthday package we offer your own personal guide that takes you and your friends through the various exhibition spaces where you get to try out painting with VR glasses, feel the Newtonian physics from our bumper swing and much more!

For more information, please contact us.

Rent conference room

We offer a conference room with more than 200 seats for job gatherings after the museum's official closing time.

If you want some fun and socialize with your colleagues after the conference is over, we can offer you guided tours around the museum.

If you are interested and want more information or/and book a date, please don't hesitate to contact us.
