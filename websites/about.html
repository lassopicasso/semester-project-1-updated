About us

The Community Science Museum

Since we started in 1994, the aim of our museum has been to create a space where everyone can experience the wonders of our discoveries and perhaps even ignite a lifelong passion to continue the explaration of the world around us.

We believe science should not be confined to the textbook, but brought to live through exhibits. This is why why have over 1000 different exhibits on the many varied subjects of science to explore. Many of these exhibits are designed for you to interact with and play around to see science come to life (apart from the dinosaur exhibits - they only come to life at night when everyone's gone home).
