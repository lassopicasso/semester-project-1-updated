Visiting Professor of Aeronautics

Date: February 9th 17:00

It is our pleasure to announce that Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting 'space'.

After the lectures there will be arranged a Q&A where everyone has the opportunity to ask Prof Sheila questions about this topic.

Night in the Museum

Date: February 20th 21:00

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays.

Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready for an exciting night with midtnight entertainment - and who knows, maybe some objects in the museum will come to alive.

Energetica Exhibition on Loan

Date: March 10th - March 30th

On a loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum. It's a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them.

From solar energy powering lightning, to 'Wind Island' thats shows how turbines are able to use and control wind to create power.
