Explore together

Come join us at the Community Science Museum where we're committed to making science accessible to all.

Everyone are welcome to our community museum. However, our main focus are the children, where we offer them an experience on how fun science really can be. We offer guided tours as well, so parents and teachers, take a trip to us and have a great day!
