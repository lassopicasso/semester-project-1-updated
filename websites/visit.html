Admission

The entrance is free for all!

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organize a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Accessibility

We have facilities so that everyone can visit our museum

There are accessible parking spaces right at the entrance of the museum.

The museum also has wheelchair accessibility ramps and we can offer audio guides and braille display for the visually impaired

Food and Drink

If you become hungry of thirsty during your visit, dont worry, we have you covered.

There is a cafe attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more

Shop

For the experimentalists!

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you've left the museum.
